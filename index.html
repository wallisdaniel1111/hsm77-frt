THE LOGIC BEHIND THE PLAN
BACKGROUND

SABERHAWKS77 is built upon the professional framework of Daniel Wallis, BA, an ALL-NAVY cross-country team member, former Nike, Inc. Professional Track & Field athlete, and multiple New Zealand representative.

The Allostatic Advantage

Most programs ignore "Life Stress." We utilize the McEwen Model of Allostatic Load to audit readiness. The program pivots in real-time, ensuring high intensities are only used when the physiology is actually capable of adapting.

Environmental Intelligence

We apply Astrand-Ryhming thermal corrections and wind-drag coefficients for NAF Atsugi.

The Sequence Science
  • Plank First: Builds Proximal Stiffness (Blagrove et al., 2018).
  • Push-ups Second: Induces Lactate Shuttling (Tillaar, 2019).
  • The Run: Execution under pre-existing metabolic load.
