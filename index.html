THE LOGIC BEHIND THE PLAN

BACKGROUND: SABERHAWKS77 is built upon the professional framework of Daniel Wallis, an ALL-NAVY team member and former NIKE Professional athlete. This program scales elite-level track and field preparation into a high-performance protocol specifically for the Navy PRT.

The Allostatic Advantage: Utilizing the McEwen Model of Allostatic Load to audit readiness. The program pivots in real-time based on sleep and stress inputs, ensuring high intensities are only prescribed when the physiology is capable of adaptation.

✕

6-WEEK TACTICAL ROADMAP

WEEK 1: ADAPTATION

Neural Priming. Teaching the brain goal pace rhythm.

WEEK 2: LOADING

Structural Stress. Building mitochondrial density and aerobic strength.

WEEK 3: DEVELOPMENT

Threshold Expansion. Moving the "red line" further out.

WEEK 4: OVERREACHING

Peak Load. Maximum growth stimulus and allostatic pressure.

WEEK 5: REALIZATION

Intensity Maintenance. Holding speed while shedding junk volume.

WEEK 6: ABSORPTION

The Taper. Shedding fatigue to reveal your Day 42 Peak.
