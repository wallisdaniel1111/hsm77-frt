6-WEEK TACTICAL ROADMAP
WEEK 1: ADAPTATION

Neural Priming. Teaching the brain the rhythm of goal pace while minimizing musculoskeletal shock.

WEEK 2: LOADING

Structural Stress. Building mitochondrial density and capillary beds to support future high-intensity output.

WEEK 3: DEVELOPMENT

Threshold Expansion. Moving the "red line" further out by increasing the duration of intervals at PRT goal pace.

WEEK 4: OVERREACHING

Peak Load. Maximum growth stimulus. This week induces the highest allostatic pressure to force systemic adaptation.

WEEK 5: REALIZATION

Intensity Maintenance. Holding top-end speed while shedding "junk volume" to begin the recovery process.

WEEK 6: ABSORPTION

The Taper. Slashing volume by 60% to shed fatigue and reveal the physiological gains hidden by Week 4 loading.
