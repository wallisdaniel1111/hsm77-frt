THE LOGIC BEHIND THE PLAN

BACKGROUND: SABERHAWKS77 is built upon the professional framework of Daniel Wallis, a multiple New Zealand representative, ALL-NAVY team member, and former NIKE Professional Track & Field athlete. This program translates elite-level championship preparation into a scalable protocol for the Navy PRT.

The Allostatic Advantage: Most programs ignore "Life Stress." We utilize the McEwen Model of Allostatic Load to audit readiness. The program pivots in real-time, ensuring high intensities are only used when the physiology is actually capable of adapting.

Environmental Intelligence: We apply Astrand-Ryhming thermal corrections and wind-drag coefficients for NAF Atsugi. This ensures relative effort remains constant regardless of external friction.

The Sequence Science:

  • Plank First: Builds Proximal Stiffness to eliminate energy leaks (Blagrove et al., 2018).
  • Push-ups Second: Induces Lactate Shuttling before the run (Tillaar, 2019).
  • The Run: Execution under pre-existing metabolic load.

THE PHYSIOLOGY OF THE TAPER: The taper is a clinical unmasking of performance. By slashing volume 60% in Week 6, we trigger:

  • Mitochondrial Enzyme Peak: Maximizing ATP production via Citrate Synthase activity.
  • Glycogen Supercompensation: Elevating muscle fuel stores 15% above baseline for maximum output.
  • Hematological Recovery: Normalizing Red Blood Cell volume and Hemoglobin suppressed during heavy loading weeks.
